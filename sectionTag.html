Nepali Laders

Khadga Prasad Sharma Oli (Nepali: खड्ग प्रसाद शर्मा ओली; born 22 February 1952), more commonly known as K. P. Oli, is a Nepali politician who served as the 38th Prime Minister of Nepal from 11 October 2015 to 3 August 2016.[1] A member of the Communist Party of Nepal (Unified Marxist–Leninist), he was the second Prime Minister under the newly adopted Constitution of Nepal.
